My first presentation! Applied cybersecurity

Showing my first presentation i gave for fresh high school graduates to show them some practical examples.
Youssef Hesham 2023-08-19 1 min read unlisted Permalink
  • #presentation
  • #cybersecurity

My talk was a part of codebox event, it was an intro to tech careers type of event. half of the introduction to cybersecurity talk was about cybersecurity in general and the other half is me showing of what (some) cybersecurity engineers do, i want it to be cool not boring so i showed vulnerabilities like html injection, xss and tools like ghidra and burpsuite.

it was directed to high school graduates. i made it as simple as i can so it’s easy for a person with no any knowledge about tech to get an idea of what’s it all about.
